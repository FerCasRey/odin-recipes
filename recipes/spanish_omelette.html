Spanish Omelette

Description

Spanish omelette is a typical dish from Spanish cuisine. It was born due to the lack of food during harsh times, since eggs and potatoes were usual products among farmers

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 600g of potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

For 2/3 people, get:

  1. Peel and cut in quarters the potatoes.
  2. Cut those potato quartes into slices 2/3mm wide.
  3. Clean, dry and fry in olive oil those potatoes until they look gold-ish.
  4. Mix the warm potatoes with the whisked eggs and add as much salt as you wish.
  5. Wait for 5/10 minutes and add the mix to a warm pan with a drop of olive oil. The fire must me set to high.
  6. Wait for a couple minutes and make sure the bottom and the sides don't stick to the pan.
  7. Hit gently the inner part so the egg does not set too much.
  8. Now, with the help of a big plate, turn around the mix and repeat the process until the egg is cooked at your taste.
