Avocado paste

Description

This plain rice recipe is perfect for good canvas that will allow you to do any other recipe.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water

Steps

For one person:

  1. Using a measuring device, get 1.5 parts of water for every 1 of rice.
  2. Set the water and rice to boil in medium/high
  3. When it boils, turn the heat down to medium/low and put the lid on. Wait for 13 minutes.
  4. With the lid still on, get the rice off the stove and wait 10 minutes.
  5. Finally, fluff the rice and you got yourself a nice plain rice canvas!
