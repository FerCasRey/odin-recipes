Avocado paste

Description

This home-brewed avocado paste recipe is one of my favorites. It is perfect for any of those dinners which you don't want to work too hard to get a good result.

Ingredients

  • One avocado
  • One can of tuna
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Toast or crackers

Steps

For one person:

  1. Peel and cut in small cube the avocado.
  2. Smash the avocado until it becomes an uniform paste.
  3. Add salt and olive oil to your liking
  4. Open the tuna and fix with the paste in a bowl.
  5. Finally, get the toast or the crackers and put the paste on them, and you got yourself a tasty dinner!
